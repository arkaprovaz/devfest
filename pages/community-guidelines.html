Code of Conduct


Act with integrity

We must follow the guidelines of the GDG community, act with integrity and honesty in all matters and be accountable for the actions.


Comply with the code

We must comply with all applicable rules, laws and regulations during the entire event.


Be accountable

We will be held accountable for our own actions.


Be inclusive

We welcome and support people of all backgrounds and identities. This includes, but is not limited to members of any sexual orientation, gender identity and expression, race, ethnicity, culture, national origin, social and economic class, educational level, color, immigration status, sex, age, size, family status, political belief, religion, and mental and physical ability.


Be respectful

We won't all agree all the time, but disagreement is no excuse for disrespectful behavior.


As we are all a part of an ever-increasing community of tech enthusiasts and developers, we expect everyone who are joining the community to follow the above mentioned guidelines, violation of which may result in being removed from the program or even be banned from future events.
